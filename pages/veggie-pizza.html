Veggie Pizza Recipe

Veggie Pizza is a delicious and colorful way to enjoy your favorite pizza, loaded with fresh vegetables and topped with melty mozzarella cheese. This pizza is a great choice for vegetarians or anyone looking for a lighter, veggie-packed option. The combination of juicy tomatoes, sweet bell peppers, earthy mushrooms, and tangy onions creates a mouthwatering flavor that will leave you craving more. Whether you're looking for a healthier pizza or just love veggies, this Veggie Pizza is sure to be a hit!

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough: (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1 ½ cups warm water
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
    • 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Pizza Sauce: ½ cup (or your favorite pizza sauce)
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 2 cups, shredded
  • Bell Peppers: 1, thinly sliced
  • Red Onion: ½, thinly sliced
  • Mushrooms: 1 cup, sliced
  • Cherry Tomatoes: ½ cup, halved
  • Olives: ¼ cup, sliced
  • Basil: fresh, chopped for garnish
  • Olive Oil: for brushing
  • Oregano: for seasoning (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: (if making homemade)
    • In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    • Add flour, olive oil, and salt. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise for about 1 hour until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  3. Prepare the Vegetables: Slice the bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Roll Out the Dough:
    • Punch down the dough and divide it into two portions if you want two smaller pizzas or one large.
    • On a lightly floured surface, roll the dough into a circle or rectangle, depending on your preference.
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet or pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
  5. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Brush the edges of the dough lightly with olive oil.
    • Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving about a ½-inch border.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
    • Arrange the vegetables: bell peppers, red onion, mushrooms, cherry tomatoes, and olives.
    • Sprinkle oregano for extra flavor, if desired.
  6. Bake the Pizza: Bake the pizza for about 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Garnish and Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and sprinkle with freshly chopped basil.
    • Slice and enjoy your veggie pizza!
