PASTA & NOODLES

"Pasta, a beloved staple in many cuisines, is a versatile food made from durum wheat semolina or flour and water. Originating from Italy, pasta has become a global favorite, enjoyed in various forms, from simple spaghetti to intricate lasagna. The basic types of pasta include long strands like spaghetti and fettuccine, as well as shapes like penne, fusilli, and farfalle. Each shape is designed to pair well with specific types of sauces and ingredients, enhancing the overall dining experience"
