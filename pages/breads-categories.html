BREADS & PASTRIES

"Breads and pastries are delightful baked goods enjoyed around the world. From the crusty, aromatic loaves of bread to the buttery, flaky layers of pastries, these items add a special touch to any meal or occasion. Popular breads include hearty varieties like sourdough and ciabatta, while pastries offer a range of sweet and savory options, such as croissants and danishes. Each type brings unique flavors and textures, enhancing the overall dining experience."
