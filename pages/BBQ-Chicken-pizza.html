BBQ Chicken Pizza Recipe

BBQ Chicken Pizza is a delicious twist on the classic pizza, combining the smoky, tangy flavor of barbecue sauce with tender chicken and melted cheese. This pizza is perfect for those who love a blend of sweet and savory flavors. The crust is crispy and golden, topped with a generous layer of BBQ sauce, succulent pieces of chicken, and a sprinkle of cheese. Garnished with fresh cilantro and optional bacon or smoked Gouda, this pizza is sure to impress your family and friends. Whether you're hosting a casual get-together or just craving a tasty homemade meal, BBQ Chicken Pizza is a crowd-pleaser that's easy to make and even easier to enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough: (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1 ½ cups warm water
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
    • 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • BBQ Sauce: ½ cup
  • Cooked Chicken Breast: (shredded or cubed, about 1 ½ cups)
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 2 cups, shredded
  • Red Onion: ½, thinly sliced
  • Cilantro: fresh, chopped for garnish
  • Olive Oil: for brushing
  • Optional:
    • Smoked Gouda: ½ cup, shredded for extra flavor
    • Bacon: 2 strips, cooked and crumbled

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: (if making homemade)
    • In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    • Add flour, olive oil, and salt. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise for about 1 hour until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  3. Prepare the Chicken: Cook the chicken breast (grilled or sautéed) and shred or cut into cubes. Toss the chicken in about 2 tablespoons of BBQ sauce.
  4. Roll Out the Dough:
    • Punch down the dough and divide it into two portions if you want two smaller pizzas or one large.
    • On a lightly floured surface, roll the dough into a circle or rectangle, depending on your preference.
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet or pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
  5. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Brush the edges of the dough lightly with olive oil.
    • Spread a thin layer of BBQ sauce over the pizza dough, leaving about a ½-inch border.
    • Evenly distribute the shredded BBQ chicken on top of the sauce.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella (and smoked Gouda if using) over the chicken.
    • Add thinly sliced red onions over the cheese.
  6. Bake the Pizza: Bake the pizza for about 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Garnish and Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and sprinkle with freshly chopped cilantro.
    • Slice and enjoy!
