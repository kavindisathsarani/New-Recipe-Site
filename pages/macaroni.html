Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

Macaroni and Cheese is a classic comfort food that combines tender elbow macaroni with a creamy, cheesy sauce. This dish is a favorite for its rich flavor and comforting texture. Whether you're serving it as a side dish or a main course, Macaroni and Cheese is always a crowd-pleaser. With a crispy topping and gooey cheese, this recipe is perfect for cozy dinners or special occasions!

Ingredients:

  • Elbow Macaroni: 2 cups (dry)
  • Cheddar Cheese: 2 cups, shredded
  • Milk: 2 cups
  • Butter: 4 tablespoons
  • All-Purpose Flour: 2 tablespoons
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Black Pepper: ½ teaspoon
  • Paprika: ½ teaspoon (optional)
  • Bread Crumbs: ½ cup (for topping)
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese: ¼ cup (for topping)
  • Garlic Powder: ¼ teaspoon (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Macaroni:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add the elbow macaroni and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Sauce:
    • In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Stir in the flour and cook for about 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
    • Slowly whisk in the milk, ensuring there are no lumps. Continue to cook until the mixture thickens.
    • Add the shredded cheddar cheese, salt, black pepper, and paprika (if using). Stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  3. Combine and Bake:
    • Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir to coat.
    • Transfer the macaroni and cheese mixture to a greased baking dish.
    • In a small bowl, combine the bread crumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, and garlic powder (if using). Sprinkle evenly over the macaroni and cheese.
    • Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  4. Serve: Allow the macaroni and cheese to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your creamy and delicious Macaroni and Cheese!
