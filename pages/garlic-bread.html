Garlic Bread Recipe

Garlic Bread is a classic and beloved side dish, known for its crispy texture and aromatic garlic flavor. Made with fresh or toasted bread, garlic, and butter, this recipe transforms simple ingredients into a delicious accompaniment for pasta dishes, soups, or just as a tasty snack. Whether you prefer it soft or crispy, this garlic bread is sure to be a hit with family and friends.

Ingredients:

  • Baguette or Italian Bread: 1 loaf
  • Butter: ½ cup (115g), softened
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Parsley: 2 tablespoons, chopped (optional)
  • Salt: ¼ teaspoon
  • Black Pepper: ¼ teaspoon
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese: ¼ cup (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Garlic Butter:
    • In a medium bowl, mix together softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
  2. Prepare the Bread:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cut the loaf of bread in half lengthwise.
    • Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut sides of the bread.
    • If using, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top of the buttered bread.
  3. Bake the Bread:
    • Place the prepared bread on a baking sheet.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the butter has melted.
    • For extra crispiness, you can broil the bread for an additional 1-2 minutes, keeping a close eye to prevent burning.
  4. Serve: Slice and serve the garlic bread warm, and enjoy as a delicious accompaniment to your meal!
