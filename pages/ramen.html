Ramen Recipe

Ramen is a beloved Japanese noodle soup that combines rich, flavorful broth with tender noodles and a variety of toppings. This recipe brings together a savory miso or soy-based broth with ramen noodles, fresh vegetables, and your choice of protein. It's a comforting and customizable dish that's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal. Enjoy the umami-packed flavors and aromatic ingredients that make ramen a favorite worldwide!

Ingredients:

  • Ramen Noodles: 2 packs (fresh or instant)
  • Chicken or Vegetable Broth: 4 cups (960ml)
  • Soy Sauce: 3 tablespoons
  • Miso Paste: 2 tablespoons (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1 tablespoon, grated
  • Green Onions: 2, sliced
  • Baby Spinach or Bok Choy: 1 cup
  • Carrots: 1, julienned
  • Mushrooms: 1 cup, sliced (shiitake or button mushrooms)
  • Eggs: 2, soft-boiled (optional)
  • Cooked Chicken or Tofu: 1 cup, sliced or cubed
  • Sesame Oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Sriracha or Chili Paste: to taste (optional, for heat)
  • Nori (Seaweed): for garnish (optional)
  • Sesame Seeds: for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Broth:
    • In a large pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook until fragrant.
    • Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, soy sauce, and miso paste (if using). Bring to a simmer.
  2. Cook the Noodles:
    • While the broth is simmering, cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Vegetables:
    • Add the sliced mushrooms, julienned carrots, and green onions to the simmering broth. Cook for 5 minutes until vegetables are tender.
    • Add the baby spinach or bok choy and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Assemble the Ramen:
    • Divide the cooked noodles between bowls.
    • Ladle the hot broth and vegetables over the noodles.
    • Top with sliced cooked chicken or tofu, and halved soft-boiled eggs (if using).
    • Add a drizzle of sriracha or chili paste for heat, if desired.
    • Garnish with nori, sesame seeds, and additional green onions if desired.
  5. Serve: Enjoy your homemade ramen hot and fresh!
