Croissant Recipe

Croissants are a beloved French pastry known for their flaky, buttery layers. This recipe guides you through making these delightful pastries from scratch. With a bit of patience and attention to detail, you can enjoy homemade croissants with a perfect golden-brown exterior and a tender, airy interior. Ideal for breakfast or a special treat, croissants are a classic favorite that pair wonderfully with a variety of fillings or can be enjoyed plain.

Ingredients:

  • All-Purpose Flour: 4 cups (500g)
  • Sugar: ¼ cup (50g)
  • Salt: 1 ½ teaspoons
  • Instant Yeast: 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet)
  • Milk: 1 cup (240ml), warm
  • Butter: ½ cup (115g), melted
  • Eggs: 2 large
  • Unsalted Butter: 1 cup (230g), cold and cubed, for laminating
  • Egg Wash: 1 egg, beaten, for brushing

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast.
    • Add warm milk, melted butter, and eggs. Stir until the dough starts to come together.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth.
    • Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the Butter Layer:
    • Place the cold, cubed butter between two sheets of parchment paper.
    • Use a rolling pin to flatten the butter into a thin, even rectangle.
    • Refrigerate the butter until firm.
  3. Laminate the Dough:
    • On a floured surface, roll out the dough into a large rectangle.
    • Place the chilled butter layer on one half of the dough, then fold the other half over it.
    • Roll the dough out into a large rectangle again, then fold it into thirds (like a letter).
    • Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
    • Repeat the rolling and folding process two more times, chilling the dough between each fold.
  4. Shape the Croissants:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Roll the dough out into a large rectangle and cut it into triangles.
    • Starting from the wide end, roll each triangle into a crescent shape.
    • Place the shaped croissants on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    • Brush with egg wash.
  5. Bake:
    • Bake the croissants in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown and crisp.
    • Allow to cool slightly before serving.
