Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its rich layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy béchamel. This hearty and satisfying dish is perfect for family dinners or special occasions. The combination of tender noodles, savory meat sauce, and gooey cheese creates a deliciously comforting meal. Whether you’re a lasagna enthusiast or new to this dish, this recipe will surely become a favorite!

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Noodles: 12 sheets (no-boil or regular)
  • Ground Beef: 1 pound (450g), cooked and crumbled
  • Onion: 1, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Tomato Sauce: 2 cups (480ml)
  • Tomato Paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Ricotta Cheese: 1 cup (250g)
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 2 cups (200g), shredded
  • Parmesan Cheese: ½ cup (50g), grated
  • Egg: 1, beaten
  • Basil: 1 teaspoon, dried
  • Oregano: 1 teaspoon, dried
  • Salt: to taste
  • Pepper: to taste
  • Olive Oil: 1 tablespoon

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
    • Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
    • Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, beaten egg, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread some of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
    • Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake the Lasagna: Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  6. Cool and Serve: Let the lasagna cool for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
