Chocolate Mousse Recipe

Chocolate mousse is a luxurious and creamy dessert that's both rich and light. This recipe combines high-quality chocolate with whipped cream to create a velvety mousse that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're serving it as a special treat or a sophisticated dessert, this mousse is sure to impress with its smooth texture and intense chocolate flavor.

Ingredients:

  • For the Mousse:
    • 4 ounces (115g) semisweet or bittersweet chocolate, chopped
    • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
    • 2 large eggs, separated
    • Â¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • For Garnish (optional):
    • Whipped cream
    • Shaved chocolate or chocolate curls
    • Fresh berries

Instructions:

  1. Melt the Chocolate:
    • In a heatproof bowl, combine the chopped chocolate and butter. Set the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) and stir until the chocolate and butter are melted and smooth. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  2. Prepare the Egg Yolks:
    • In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks and granulated sugar until pale and slightly thickened. Stir in the melted chocolate mixture and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Whip the Cream:
    • In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form.
    • Gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture until fully incorporated.
  4. Whip the Egg Whites:
    • In a clean bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
    • Gently fold the egg whites into the chocolate mixture in thirds, being careful not to deflate the mousse.
  5. Chill the Mousse:
    • Spoon the mousse into individual serving dishes or glasses. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until set.
  6. Serve:
    • Top with whipped cream, chocolate shavings, and fresh berries if desired. Enjoy your rich and creamy chocolate mousse!
