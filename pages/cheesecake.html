Cheesecake Recipe

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert that combines a smooth cheese filling with a crisp, buttery crust. This recipe features a classic New York-style cheesecake with a graham cracker crust and a velvety cream cheese filling. It's perfect for special occasions or as a delightful treat any time. This cheesecake can be topped with a variety of fruits or sauces for added flavor and visual appeal.

Ingredients:

  • For the Crust:
    • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
    • ¼ cup granulated sugar
    • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • For the Filling:
    • 4 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
    • 1 cup granulated sugar
    • 4 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 cup sour cream
    • 1 cup heavy cream
  • For the Topping (optional):
    • Fresh fruit (e.g., strawberries, blueberries)
    • Fruit compote or sauce

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Crust:
    • Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
    • In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix until well combined.
    • Press the mixture evenly into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust. Bake for 10 minutes, then let cool.
  2. Prepare the Filling:
    • In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
    • Add sugar and beat until well combined.
    • Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
    • Mix in sour cream and heavy cream until smooth.
    • Pour the filling over the cooled crust in the springform pan.
    • Bake for 60-70 minutes, or until the center is set and the edges are lightly browned. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool inside the oven with the door slightly ajar for 1 hour.
    • Remove from the oven and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to fully set.
  3. Prepare the Topping (optional):
    • Top with fresh fruit or fruit compote before serving.
  4. Serve: Slice and enjoy your homemade cheesecake chilled. It's perfect for any occasion!
