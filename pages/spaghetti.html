Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish featuring a rich and savory meat sauce served over spaghetti noodles. This hearty and comforting dish is perfect for family dinners or casual gatherings. The flavorful sauce, made with ground beef, tomatoes, and aromatic vegetables, pairs beautifully with tender spaghetti. Whether you're cooking for a crowd or just enjoying a cozy meal, Spaghetti Bolognese is sure to satisfy!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti: 12 ounces (340g)
  • Ground Beef: 1 pound (450g)
  • Onion: 1, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 3 cloves, minced
  • Carrots: 2, diced
  • Celery: 2 stalks, diced
  • Tomato Sauce: 1 can (15 ounces or 425g)
  • Tomato Paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Beef Broth: 1 cup (240ml)
  • Red Wine: Â½ cup (120ml) (optional)
  • Italian Seasoning: 1 tablespoon
  • Basil: 1 teaspoon, dried
  • Oregano: 1 teaspoon, dried
  • Salt: to taste
  • Pepper: to taste
  • Olive Oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Parmesan Cheese: for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Spaghetti:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add the spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat.
    • Add chopped onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Cook until vegetables are softened.
    • Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
    • Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, beef broth, and red wine (if using).
    • Add Italian seasoning, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Combine and Serve:
    • Toss the cooked spaghetti with the meat sauce until well coated.
    • Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
