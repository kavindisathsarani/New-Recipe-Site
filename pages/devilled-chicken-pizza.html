Devilled Chicken Pizza Recipe

Devilled Chicken Pizza is a fiery and flavorful take on your favorite pizza, inspired by the bold and spicy flavors of the classic devilled chicken. This pizza packs a punch with its spicy marinated chicken, tangy tomato sauce, and melty cheese, all layered on a crispy crust. The combination of heat and savory goodness makes it a perfect choice for those who love a little extra kick in their meal. Topped with onions, bell peppers, and a sprinkle of chili flakes, this pizza brings the heat while delivering a mouthwatering balance of flavors. Whether you're a fan of spicy food or just looking to try something exciting, Devilled Chicken Pizza is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough: (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1 ½ cups warm water
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
    • 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Spicy Chicken Marinade:
    • 2 chicken breasts, shredded or cubed
    • 2 tablespoons chili powder
    • 1 tablespoon paprika
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon vinegar
    • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
    • 1 tablespoon red chili flakes (adjust for heat)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tomato Sauce: ½ cup (or your favorite pizza sauce)
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 2 cups, shredded
  • Red Onion: ½, thinly sliced
  • Bell Peppers: 1, thinly sliced
  • Cilantro: fresh, chopped for garnish
  • Olive Oil: for brushing
  • Chili Flakes: for extra heat (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough: (if making homemade)
    • In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    • Add flour, olive oil, and salt. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise for about 1 hour until doubled in size.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, mix chili powder, paprika, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic paste, red chili flakes, salt, and pepper. Toss the chicken in this marinade and let it sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  4. Cook the Chicken: Grill or sauté the marinated chicken until fully cooked, then shred or cut it into cubes.
  5. Roll Out the Dough:
    • Punch down the dough and divide it into two portions if you want two smaller pizzas or one large.
    • On a lightly floured surface, roll the dough into a circle or rectangle, depending on your preference.
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet or pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
  6. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Brush the edges of the dough lightly with olive oil.
    • Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the pizza dough, leaving about a ½-inch border.
    • Evenly distribute the spicy chicken on top of the sauce.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the chicken.
    • Add thinly sliced red onions and bell peppers over the cheese.
  7. Bake the Pizza: Bake the pizza for about 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Garnish and Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and sprinkle with freshly chopped cilantro.
    • Add extra chili flakes if desired for additional heat.
    • Slice and enjoy!
