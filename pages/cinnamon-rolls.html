Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

Cinnamon Rolls are a delicious and comforting treat, perfect for breakfast or a special dessert. This recipe features soft, fluffy rolls swirled with a rich cinnamon-sugar mixture and topped with a creamy glaze. With a bit of patience and the right techniques, you can make these delightful rolls from scratch and enjoy them warm from the oven. Whether served with a cup of coffee or enjoyed on their own, cinnamon rolls are sure to please everyone.

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:
    • All-Purpose Flour: 4 cups (500g)
    • Sugar: ¼ cup (50g)
    • Salt: 1 teaspoon
    • Instant Yeast: 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet)
    • Milk: 1 cup (240ml), warm
    • Butter: ¼ cup (60g), melted
    • Eggs: 2 large
  • For the Filling:
    • Brown Sugar: ¾ cup (150g)
    • Ground Cinnamon: 2 tablespoons
    • Butter: ¼ cup (60g), softened
  • For the Glaze:
    • Powdered Sugar: 1 cup (120g)
    • Milk: 2 tablespoons
    • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast.
    • Add warm milk, melted butter, and eggs. Stir until the dough begins to come together.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the Filling:
    • In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and softened butter until well combined.
  3. Shape the Rolls:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • On a floured surface, roll out the dough into a large rectangle.
    • Spread the cinnamon-sugar filling evenly over the dough.
    • Starting from the long edge, roll the dough tightly into a log.
    • Cut the log into 12 even slices and place them in a greased baking dish.
    • Cover with plastic wrap and let rise for about 30 minutes, or until the rolls are puffy.
  4. Bake the Rolls:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.
    • Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
  5. Prepare the Glaze:
    • In a bowl, whisk together powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.
    • Drizzle the glaze over the warm cinnamon rolls.
  6. Serve: Enjoy your homemade cinnamon rolls warm and fresh!
